National Cashflow Competition 2014 (NCF 2014) features the use of the CASHFLOW 101 board gamecreated by Robert Kiyosaki, who authored the best-seller “Rich Dad Poor Dad”. This game simulates life as a rat race and teaches practical finance knowledge on managing wealth in a simple and palatable package.

Why should you join?

· Be INTRODUCED to the fundamentals of the various investment platforms
· Be TRAINED in financial literacy-- read financial statements and manage your cashflow
· HONE your skills in accounting, wealth management and investing in a risk-free environment
· Look forward to a FUN learning experience with our enthusiastic and dynamic facilitators
· FREE Goodie Bag, ATTRACTIVE prizes, NEW friends and MANY MORE
